# ⏱️ Reaction Timer Game

A simple and fun Reaction Timer built using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**.  
The goal is to click the box as quickly as possible once it turns green.

---

## How to Play 
Click here to play the game - https://reaction-timer-sigma.vercel.app/
1. Click **Start** to begin the game.  
2. The box will say **"Get Ready..."** and turn yellow.  
3. After a random delay, it turns **green** and shows **"CLICK!"**  
4. Click immediately to record your reaction time.  
5. Your **latest** and **average** reaction times are displayed.  
6. If you click too early, it will show **"Too Soon!"**

---

##  Built With
- **HTML** – Markup  
- **CSS** – UI styling  
- **JavaScript (DOM)** – Game logic & timer
